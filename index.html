Questions we hear a lot

We'll clear things up.

How much time do I need to commit each week?+

Around 2–3 hours per week for recording. We handle everything else — ideation, scripting, editing, scheduling, and strategy.

What if I don't know what to talk about?+

That's exactly what we solve. We provide you with scripts, topics, and angles based on your expertise and audience. You just hit record.

Do I need professional equipment?+

No. A smartphone with decent lighting is enough to start. We'll guide you on simple setup improvements as we go.

How quickly will I see results?+

Most clients see a noticeable increase in engagement and inbound leads within the first 4–6 weeks of consistent posting.

What happens after the 12 weeks?+

You can continue with a maintenance plan, or take the system we built and run it yourself. Either way, you leave with a repeatable content engine.

Do I have to dance on camera?+

Absolutely not. We build authority content, not trend-chasing fluff. You talk naturally into the camera — no mirror dances, no awkward pointing, no embarrassing formats. Just you sharing your knowledge.
